Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken fried rice i a very common dish in many asian countries particularly Chinese and Thai people love eating fried rice . Cooked rice,diced or shredded chicken,eggs,vegetables,and seasonings are usually stir-fried together in a pan to create a flavorful dish . This is a vey tasty and flavorful recipe that you can make with any veggies,spices and protein to suit an indivisual taste.

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken fillets 250g
  • Cooking oil 1 & ½ tbs
  • Anday (Eggs) whisked 3
  • Cooking oil 2 tbs
  • Lehsan (Garlic) chopped 1 tbs
  • Gajar (Carrot) julienne ½ Cup
  • Shimla mirch (Capsicum) julienne ½ Cup
  • gobhi (Cabbage) 3/4 Cup
  • Sugar ½ tsp
  • Namak (Salt) 1/4 tsp or to taste
  • Chawal (Rice) basmati ½ kg (soaked & boiled with salt)
  • Knorr chicken powder 1 tbs
  • Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) 1 tsp
  • Safed mirch powder (White pepper powder) ½ tsp
  • Sirka (Vinegar) 1 tbs
  • Soy sauce 1 & ½ tbs
  • Hara pyaz (Green onion) leaves ½ Cup
  • Hara pyaz (Green onion) leaves

Directions

  1. Cut chicken fillet into thin strips & set aside.
  2. In wok,add cooking oil and eggs,mix well and cook until crumbled & set aside.
  3. In the same wok,add cooking oil,garlic and fry until light golden.
  4. Add chicken and mix well until it changes color and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add carrot,capsicum and mix well for a minute.
  6. Add cabbage and mix well.
  7. Add sugar,salt and mix well.
  8. Add boiled rice and give it a good mix.
  9. Add chicken powder and mix well.
  10. Add black pepper powder,white pepper powder,vinegar,soy sauce and mix well.
  11. Now add eggs,green onion leaves and mix.
  12. Garnish with green onion leaves & serve!
